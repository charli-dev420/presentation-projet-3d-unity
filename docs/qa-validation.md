# QA Validation / Validation QA

[EN](#english) | [FR](#francais)

![QA matrix](../assets/qa-matrix.svg)

## English

### QA Model

Quality is checked at four moments: source review, candidate preparation, Unity handoff, and final decision. Each moment should produce a readable note.

For **source review**, check framing, clarity, duplicates, rights status, subject usefulness, and asset potential.

For **Splat Face preparation**, check silhouette, facade suitability, depth-card usefulness, texture readability, scene role, and mobile constraint.

For **Unity handoff**, check import, naming, scale, orientation, pivot, bounds, material state, texture expectations, and platform weight.

For **final decision**, write accept, revise, reject, archive, or revisit with the reason.

### Positive Framing

QA is not a way to close doors. It is the shared language that lets someone improve the source, change the asset route, or choose a better validation target.

## Francais

### Modele QA

La qualite se controle a quatre moments: revue source, preparation candidat, handoff Unity et decision finale. Chaque moment doit produire une note lisible.

Pour **revue source**, controler cadrage, clarte, doublons, droits, utilite sujet et potentiel asset.

Pour **preparation Splat Face**, controler silhouette, pertinence facade, utilite depth-card, lisibilite texture, role scene et contrainte mobile.

Pour **handoff Unity**, controler import, nommage, echelle, orientation, pivot, bounds, etat materiau, attentes texture et poids plateforme.

Pour **decision finale**, ecrire accepter, reviser, refuser, archiver ou revoir plus tard avec la raison.

### Formulation Positive

La QA ne sert pas a fermer des portes. C'est le langage partage qui permet d'ameliorer la source, changer la route asset ou choisir une meilleure cible de validation.
