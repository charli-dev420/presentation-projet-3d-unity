# Project Evaluation / Evaluation projet

[EN](#english) | [FR](#francais)

## English

### How To Judge The Project

Judge the project by the quality of the decision it produces. A strong Splat Face workflow should let another person understand the source, the asset route, the Unity/mobile constraints, the checks applied, and the reason to accept, revise, reject, or archive the candidate.

The best evaluation is small and concrete: one source set, one facade or 2.5D route, one Unity-facing check, and one written decision.

### What To Review First

Start with [Splat Face](projects/splat-face.md), [overview](overview.md), [validation scenarios](validation-scenarios.md), and [QA validation](qa-validation.md). Then review [Dataset ReviewEval](projects/dataset-revieweval.md), [CodexUnity / CodexToUnity](projects/codexunity-codextounity.md), [Mob'ia / ccomf-unity](projects/mobia-ccomf-unity.md), and [LocalAssetFactory](projects/local-asset-factory.md).

### Strong Signals

- The source decision is written before asset preparation.
- The asset candidate has a target role and platform constraint.
- Unity checks are separated from visual preference.
- The review result is understandable without a live explanation.
- The next useful support area is clear.

### Useful Support Areas

Unity import QA, mobile constraints, facade and 2.5D asset criteria, dataset review, technical-art feedback, product workflow review, documentation, funding, missions, and roles around real-time production.

## Francais

### Comment Juger Le Projet

Juger le projet par la qualite de la decision produite. Un workflow Splat Face solide doit permettre a une autre personne de comprendre la source, la route asset, les contraintes Unity/mobile, les controles appliques et la raison d'accepter, reviser, refuser ou archiver le candidat.

La meilleure evaluation est petite et concrete: un lot source, une route facade ou 2.5D, un controle oriente Unity et une decision ecrite.

### Quoi Lire En Premier

Commencer par [Splat Face](projects/splat-face.md), [overview](overview.md), [validation scenarios](validation-scenarios.md) et [QA validation](qa-validation.md). Lire ensuite [Dataset ReviewEval](projects/dataset-revieweval.md), [CodexUnity / CodexToUnity](projects/codexunity-codextounity.md), [Mob'ia / ccomf-unity](projects/mobia-ccomf-unity.md) et [LocalAssetFactory](projects/local-asset-factory.md).

### Signaux Forts

- La decision source est ecrite avant la preparation asset.
- Le candidat asset a un role cible et une contrainte plateforme.
- Les controles Unity sont separes de la preference visuelle.
- Le resultat de revue est comprehensible sans explication en direct.
- La prochaine zone de support utile est claire.

### Zones De Support Utiles

QA import Unity, contraintes mobile, criteres asset facade et 2.5D, revue dataset, feedback technical-art, revue workflow produit, documentation, financement, missions et postes autour de la production temps reel.
