### Interpretation

Les preuves publiques montrent un ecosysteme multi-repos, oriente production et centre sur des criteres d'utilisation Unity. Elles servent de dossier d'entree pour demo ciblee, partenariat ou audit cadre.

## English

Public evidence shows a real multi-repository ecosystem focused on dataset evaluation, asset generation or baking, and Unity usability. It is designed as an entry dossier for a scoped demo, partnership discussion, or bounded technical review.
