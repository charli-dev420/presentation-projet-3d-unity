## English

### Evidence Summary

The evidence model follows the pipeline. A claim is useful only when it tells the reader what was reviewed and what decision can be made from it.

**Dataset ReviewEval** provides source-review evidence: selection criteria, exports, review notes, and keep/fix/reject decisions.

**Splat Face / Splat Facade Baker** provides asset-route evidence: facade or 2.5D candidate criteria, expected Unity role, mobile constraints, and visual readability.

**CodexUnity / CodexToUnity** provides handoff evidence: request framing, manifest expectations, candidate naming, and Unity import checks.

**Mob'ia / ccomf-unity** provides product workflow evidence: profile, job, artifact, client surface, state, and next action.

**LocalAssetFactory** provides local readiness evidence: normalization, manifest, import expectation, and review status.

### Proof Cards

`3D-EV-01` - Source review can be explained before asset work begins. Read through Dataset ReviewEval notes and source facts.

`3D-EV-02` - Splat Face has a concrete lightweight Unity/mobile direction. Read through Splat Face, project map, and QA validation.

`3D-EV-03` - Unity handoff needs manifests and acceptance criteria. Read through CodexUnity / CodexToUnity and LocalAssetFactory.

`3D-EV-04` - Product workflow needs readable job and artifact states. Read through Mob'ia / ccomf-unity and user flows.

### Interpretation

The strongest proof is not a broad claim. It is a readable chain from source decision to asset candidate to Unity-facing review result.

## Francais

### Synthese Des Preuves

Le modele de preuve suit le pipeline. Un claim est utile seulement s'il dit au lecteur ce qui a ete revu et quelle decision peut en sortir.

**Dataset ReviewEval** apporte la preuve de revue source: criteres de selection, exports, notes de revue et decisions garder/corriger/refuser.

**Splat Face / Splat Facade Baker** apporte la preuve de route asset: criteres candidat facade ou 2.5D, role Unity attendu, contraintes mobile et lisibilite visuelle.

**CodexUnity / CodexToUnity** apporte la preuve de handoff: cadrage demande, attentes manifest, nommage candidat et controles import Unity.

**Mob'ia / ccomf-unity** apporte la preuve de workflow produit: profil, job, artefact, surface client, etat et prochaine action.

**LocalAssetFactory** apporte la preuve de readiness locale: normalisation, manifest, attente import et statut de revue.

### Cartes De Preuve

`3D-EV-01` - La revue source peut etre expliquee avant le travail asset. Lire Dataset ReviewEval et source facts.

`3D-EV-02` - Splat Face a une direction Unity/mobile legere concrete. Lire Splat Face, project map et QA validation.

`3D-EV-03` - Le handoff Unity demande manifests et criteres d'acceptation. Lire CodexUnity / CodexToUnity et LocalAssetFactory.

`3D-EV-04` - Le workflow produit demande des etats job et artefact lisibles. Lire Mob'ia / ccomf-unity et user flows.

### Interpretation

La preuve la plus forte n'est pas un claim large. C'est une chaine lisible entre decision source, candidat asset et resultat de revue oriente Unity.
