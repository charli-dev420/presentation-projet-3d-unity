## English

### Product Definition

Splat Face / Splat Facade Baker is the main project in this showcase. It explores a practical 3D/Unity route between flat visual material and lightweight scene-ready candidates: facades, 2.5D cards, depth-card logic, texture readability, and mobile constraints.

The goal is not to turn every image into a final asset automatically. The goal is to make the asset path easier to judge: what source was used, what kind of candidate was prepared, what the expected Unity role is, and why the result should move forward or stop.

### Who It Helps

It helps Unity users, technical artists, small teams, AI asset reviewers, and product collaborators who need lighter visual assets, facade experiments, or mobile-friendly candidates without losing review discipline.

### Workflow

1. Select a reviewed source from Dataset ReviewEval or another cleared source set.
2. Define the target role: facade, card, prop-like visual, background dressing, or prototype asset.
3. Prepare the candidate route: framing, depth-card logic, map expectations, scale assumptions, and texture readability.
4. Check Unity-facing criteria: import, naming, bounds, materials, orientation, mobile weight, and scene usefulness.
5. Write the decision: accept, revise, reject, archive, or keep for another pass.

### What This Repository Shows

This repo explains the product framing, quality criteria, reading path, evidence model, QA checklist, project relationships, and visual material needed to understand Splat Face as a real Unity/mobile asset direction.

### Useful Support

Useful support includes Unity import review, facade asset criteria, technical-art QA, Blender/GLB inspection, mobile performance review, dataset source feedback, documentation, funding, mission work, and roles around real-time production.

## Francais

### Definition Produit

Splat Face / Splat Facade Baker est le projet principal de cette vitrine. Il explore une route 3D/Unity pratique entre materiel visuel plat et candidats legers utilisables en scene: facades, cartes 2.5D, logique depth-card, lisibilite texture et contraintes mobile.

Le but n'est pas de transformer automatiquement chaque image en asset final. Le but est de rendre la route asset plus facile a juger: quelle source a ete utilisee, quel candidat a ete prepare, quel role Unity est attendu et pourquoi le resultat doit avancer ou s'arreter.

### A Qui Ca Sert

Il sert aux utilisateurs Unity, technical artists, petites equipes, reviewers d'assets IA et collaborateurs produit qui ont besoin d'assets visuels plus legers, d'experimentations facade ou de candidats compatibles mobile sans perdre la discipline de revue.

### Workflow

1. Choisir une source revue depuis Dataset ReviewEval ou un autre lot autorise.
2. Definir le role cible: facade, card, prop visuel, habillage de scene ou asset prototype.
3. Preparer la route candidat: cadrage, logique depth-card, attentes map, hypotheses d'echelle et lisibilite texture.
4. Controler les criteres Unity: import, nommage, bounds, materiaux, orientation, poids mobile et utilite scene.
5. Ecrire la decision: accepter, reviser, refuser, archiver ou garder pour une autre passe.

### Ce Que Montre Ce Repo

Ce repo explique le cadrage produit, les criteres qualite, le chemin de lecture, le modele de preuve, la checklist QA, les relations projet et les visuels utiles pour comprendre Splat Face comme vraie direction asset Unity/mobile.

### Support Utile

Le support utile inclut revue import Unity, criteres asset facade, QA technical-art, inspection Blender/GLB, revue performance mobile, feedback source dataset, documentation, financement, missions et postes autour de la production temps reel.
