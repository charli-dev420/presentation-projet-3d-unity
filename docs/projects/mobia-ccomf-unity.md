## English

### Product Definition

Mob'ia / ccomf-unity is the product layer around generation and asset workflow jobs. It frames profiles, jobs, artifacts, review states, and Unity/web/mobile clients so the work is readable from a product surface.

In this 3D showcase, Mob'ia supports Splat Face by making job state and artifact review easier to follow. It is not the same thing as Splat Face; it is the product surface that can hold the workflow around it.

### Who It Helps

It helps users, collaborators, reviewers, and partners who need to understand what job ran, what artifact exists, what state it is in, and what should happen next.

### Workflow

1. Attach a request to a profile.
2. Track the job and expected artifact.
3. Expose state to a Unity, web, or mobile client.
4. Review the artifact with criteria from Splat Face, CodexUnity, or LocalAssetFactory.
5. Record the next action.

### What This Repository Shows

This repo shows the role of Mob'ia / ccomf-unity in the 3D workflow family: product language, job lifecycle, artifact states, clients, and relationship to Unity-facing review.

### Useful Support

Useful support includes product UX, job state naming, artifact review flows, client-facing summaries, ComfyUI profile feedback, QA wording, and documentation.

## Francais

### Definition Produit

Mob'ia / ccomf-unity est la couche produit autour des jobs de generation et de workflow asset. Elle cadre profils, jobs, artefacts, etats de revue et clients Unity/web/mobile pour que le travail soit lisible depuis une surface produit.

Dans cette vitrine 3D, Mob'ia soutient Splat Face en rendant l'etat job et la revue artefact plus faciles a suivre. Ce n'est pas la meme chose que Splat Face; c'est la surface produit qui peut porter le workflow autour.

### A Qui Ca Sert

Elle sert aux utilisateurs, collaborateurs, reviewers et partenaires qui doivent comprendre quel job a tourne, quel artefact existe, dans quel etat il est et quelle suite donner.

### Workflow

1. Attacher une demande a un profil.
2. Suivre le job et l'artefact attendu.
3. Exposer l'etat a un client Unity, web ou mobile.
4. Revoir l'artefact avec les criteres de Splat Face, CodexUnity ou LocalAssetFactory.
5. Enregistrer la prochaine action.

### Ce Que Montre Ce Repo

Ce repo montre le role de Mob'ia / ccomf-unity dans la famille workflow 3D: langage produit, cycle de vie job, etats artefact, clients et relation a la revue orientee Unity.

### Support Utile
