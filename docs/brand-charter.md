### Application 3D/Unity

- Utiliser la marque comme signal de hub, pas comme preuve technique.
- Associer l'identite DAWWW Core aux documents de pipeline et validation.
- Garder les visuels simples, lisibles et publics.
- Ne pas afficher d'assets prives comme decoration.

## English

Use DAWWW Core identity as a clear hub marker for the 3D/Unity pipeline. Do not use private generated assets, datasets, or Unity screenshots as decoration unless they have been explicitly cleared for publication.
