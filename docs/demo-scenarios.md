# Demo Scenarios / Scenarios de demonstration

[EN](#english) | [FR](#francais)

## English

| Scenario | Audience | Public-safe agenda | Success criteria | Public artifact |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Dataset review | Buyer, dataset lead, technical artist. | Use a cleared sample set, score quality, explain keep/fix/reject decisions, export a readable summary. | Another reviewer can understand the dataset decision without opening private files. | Redacted JSON/CSV/Markdown summary and decision notes. |
| Splat Face 2.5D asset route | Unity/mobile studio, technical artist. | Walk from visual source to depth-card/map expectations, then to Unity-facing acceptance criteria. | The audience knows what must be validated before the asset is useful. | Pipeline card, QA checklist, and written asset criteria. |
| CodexUnity bridge dry-run | Technical collaborator, tool reviewer. | Show job shape, manifest idea, sockets/import handoff, and normalization without private assets. | The bridge role is clear enough to choose a next live validation. | Written trace, architecture note, and import checklist. |
| Mob'ia / ccomf-unity product layer | Product partner, buyer, funder. | Walk through profile, job, artifact, review, and Unity/web/mobile client path without routes or infra details. | The product workflow can be discussed as a real client-facing tool. | User journey, integration points, and demo scope. |
| LocalAssetFactory Unity handoff | Unity reviewer, partner under NDA if needed. | Use a public or cleared asset, review preflight criteria, and document the Unity acceptance decision. | The asset is accepted, accepted with notes, or rejected for concrete reasons. | Public-safe QA result or private evidence pack. |

### Demo Rule

Every demo should start with the decision it needs to produce. A good demo answers one question cleanly: is the source usable, is the asset acceptable, is the bridge credible, or is the product flow worth deeper access?

## Francais

| Scenario | Public cible | Deroule public-safe | Criteres de succes | Artefact public |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Revue dataset | Acheteur, dataset lead, artiste technique. | Utiliser un lot autorise, scorer la qualite, expliquer garder/corriger/rejeter, exporter un resume lisible. | Un autre reviewer comprend la decision dataset sans ouvrir les fichiers prives. | Resume JSON/CSV/Markdown redige et notes de decision. |
| Route asset Splat Face 2.5D | Studio Unity/mobile, artiste technique. | Aller de la source visuelle aux attentes depth-card/maps, puis aux criteres d'acceptation Unity. | Le public sait ce qui doit etre valide avant que l'asset soit utile. | Carte pipeline, checklist QA et criteres asset rediges. |
| Dry-run pont CodexUnity | Collaborateur technique, reviewer outil. | Montrer forme de job, idee de manifest, handoff sockets/import et normalisation sans asset prive. | Le role du pont est assez clair pour choisir une validation live suivante. | Trace redigee, note architecture et checklist import. |
| Couche produit Mob'ia / ccomf-unity | Partenaire produit, acheteur, financeur. | Parcourir profil, job, artefact, revue et client Unity/web/mobile sans routes ni details infra. | Le workflow produit peut etre discute comme un vrai outil client. | Parcours utilisateur, points d'integration et scope demo. |
| Handoff Unity LocalAssetFactory | Reviewer Unity, partenaire sous NDA si besoin. | Utiliser un asset public ou autorise, revoir les criteres preflight et documenter la decision Unity. | L'asset est accepte, accepte avec notes ou rejete pour raisons concretes. | Resultat QA public-safe ou evidence pack prive. |

### Regle demo

Chaque demo doit commencer par la decision a produire. Une bonne demo repond proprement a une question: la source est-elle utilisable, l'asset est-il acceptable, le pont est-il credible ou le flow produit merite-t-il un acces plus profond?
