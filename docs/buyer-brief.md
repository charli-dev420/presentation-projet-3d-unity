# Buyer And Partner Brief / Brief acheteur et partenaire

[EN](#english) | [FR](#francais)

## English

### Value Proposition

The value is not only generation. The value is reducing the distance between AI output and a Unity-usable asset by adding source review, product orchestration, normalization, import discipline, and public-safe quality proof.

### What Is Already Concrete

- Public repositories exist for Dataset ReviewEval, Splat Facade Baker, and CodexToUnity.
- Mob'ia / ccomf-unity is framed as the product layer around ComfyUI-style profiles, jobs, artifacts, and Unity/web/mobile clients.
- LocalAssetFactory is identified as the local validation and handoff loop.
- The public dossier includes proof cards, QA matrices, user flows, buyer-facing scenarios, and visual diagrams.

### What A Buyer Should Evaluate

| Question | Why it matters | Where to start |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Can the team judge source quality before generation? | Bad input wastes model time and makes QA subjective. | [Tutorials](tutorials.md), [source facts](source-facts.md) |
| Can an asset be accepted or rejected with criteria? | Unity teams need repeatable checks, not screenshots only. | [QA validation](qa-validation.md), [proof pack](proof-pack.md) |
| Does the product layer match a real workflow? | Profiles, jobs, artifacts, and clients must fit how teams work. | [User flows](user-flows.md), [demo scenarios](demo-scenarios.md) |
| Can the next demo be scoped tightly? | A bounded demo produces a decision faster than a broad tour. | [Decision pack](decision-pack.md), [readiness notes](blockers.md) |
| Is the sharing boundary clean? | Buyers need confidence that evaluation material is clear, useful, and properly scoped. | [Public scope](public-scope.md), [license and sharing](license-and-sharing.md) |

### Good Fit

This is a good fit for buyers or partners who need Unity/mobile asset pipeline help, dataset review tooling, ComfyUI productization, technical-art QA, or a controlled evaluation of AI asset workflows.

### Not The Right Fit

This dossier is not a promise of an off-the-shelf Unity package, a hosted public backend, or unrestricted access to private generation workflows. The right next step is a scoped review or demo with agreed inputs and outputs.

## Francais

### Proposition de valeur

La valeur n'est pas seulement la generation. La valeur est de reduire l'ecart entre sortie IA et asset Unity exploitable grace a la revue des sources, l'orchestration produit, la normalisation, la discipline d'import et la preuve qualite public-safe.

### Ce qui est deja concret

- Des repos publics existent pour Dataset ReviewEval, Splat Facade Baker et CodexToUnity.
- Mob'ia / ccomf-unity est cadre comme couche produit autour de profils, jobs, artefacts et clients Unity/web/mobile lies a ComfyUI.
- LocalAssetFactory est identifie comme boucle locale de validation et handoff.
- Le dossier public contient cartes de preuve, matrices QA, flux utilisateur, scenarios acheteur et diagrammes visuels.

### Ce qu'un acheteur doit evaluer

| Question | Pourquoi cela compte | Ou commencer |
| --- | --- | --- |
| L'equipe peut-elle juger la qualite source avant generation? | Une mauvaise entree gaspille du temps modele et rend la QA subjective. | [Tutoriels](tutorials.md), [faits sources](source-facts.md) |
| Un asset peut-il etre accepte ou rejete avec criteres? | Les equipes Unity ont besoin de controles repetables, pas seulement de captures. | [QA validation](qa-validation.md), [proof pack](proof-pack.md) |
| La couche produit correspond-elle a un vrai workflow? | Profils, jobs, artefacts et clients doivent suivre le travail reel des equipes. | [Flux utilisateur](user-flows.md), [scenarios demo](demo-scenarios.md) |
| La prochaine demo peut-elle etre cadree etroite? | Une demo bornee produit une decision plus vite qu'un tour general. | [Decision pack](decision-pack.md), [notes de diligence](blockers.md) |
| La frontiere de partage est-elle propre? | Un acheteur doit savoir que le materiel d'evaluation est clair, utile et correctement cadre. | [Perimetre public](public-scope.md), [licence et partage](license-and-sharing.md) |

### Bon cas d'usage

Le projet convient aux acheteurs ou partenaires qui cherchent du pipeline asset Unity/mobile, de l'outillage revue dataset, de la productisation ComfyUI, de la QA technical-art ou une evaluation controlee de workflows assets IA.

### Mauvais cadrage

Ce dossier ne promet pas un package Unity pret a vendre, un backend public heberge ou un acces libre aux workflows de generation prives. La bonne prochaine etape est une revue ou demo ciblee avec entrees et sorties definies.
