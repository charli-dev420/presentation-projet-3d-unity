# Roadmap / Feuille de route

[EN](#english) | [FR](#francais)

## English

### Product Direction

The roadmap follows the same product line as the repo: source review, Splat Face candidate, Unity check, and written decision.

Near term: make the Splat Face story easier to inspect through clearer criteria, stronger visual examples, and a small set of validation notes.

Next: connect Dataset ReviewEval exports to Splat Face routes so source decisions visibly affect asset quality.

Then: strengthen Unity handoff through CodexUnity and LocalAssetFactory: naming, manifests, import checks, scale, pivot, bounds, materials, texture expectations, and platform weight.

Later: make Mob'ia / ccomf-unity carry the workflow in a product surface with profiles, jobs, artifacts, review states, and client summaries.

### Roadmap Principle

Every milestone should produce something another person can judge: a source decision, an asset candidate review, a Unity checklist result, or a product workflow note.

## Francais

### Direction Produit

La roadmap suit la meme ligne produit que le repo: revue source, candidat Splat Face, controle Unity et decision ecrite.

Court terme: rendre le recit Splat Face plus inspectable avec criteres plus clairs, meilleurs exemples visuels et petit lot de notes de validation.

Ensuite: connecter les exports Dataset ReviewEval aux routes Splat Face pour que les decisions source influencent visiblement la qualite asset.

Puis: renforcer le handoff Unity via CodexUnity et LocalAssetFactory: nommage, manifests, controles import, echelle, pivot, bounds, materiaux, attentes texture et poids plateforme.

Plus tard: faire porter le workflow par Mob'ia / ccomf-unity dans une surface produit avec profils, jobs, artefacts, etats de revue et resumes client.

### Principe Roadmap

Chaque jalon doit produire quelque chose qu'une autre personne peut juger: decision source, revue candidat asset, resultat checklist Unity ou note workflow produit.
